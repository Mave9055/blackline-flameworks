The Method

Hand-Built. Flame-Finished.

Every build starts with select hardwood slabs — no veneer, no fillers. The process is tuned for furniture-grade work, not siding.

01

Select Slabs

Solid hardwood only. The species sets the density, the weight, and how the fire walks the grain.

02

Open Flame

Each surface is charred with a torch, pulling the oils to the surface and darkening the winter growth.

03

Wire Brush

The soft char is brushed back by hand to reveal a three-dimensional grain pattern you can feel.

04

Hard Seal

Finished with high-grade oils or satin topcoats. It won't flake, smudge, or leave soot on your hands.

The Work

From Hand to Room-Scale.

Matched sets are planned from the first cut and built to the room they will live in. Once a slab is gone, that exact flame map is gone with it.
